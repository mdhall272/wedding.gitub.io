Our Story

Our love story began five years ago when we met at a coffee shop on a rainy Tuesday morning. Matthew was running late for work, and Aiyana was buried in a book. When Matthew accidentally bumped into Aiyana's table, spilling her coffee, it could have been a disaster. Instead, it was the beginning of our greatest adventure.

From that first conversation about literature and life, we knew we had found something special. Through late-night conversations, weekend adventures, and quiet moments at home, our love has grown stronger every day.

Now, we're ready to take the next step in our journey together, and we can't imagine celebrating this moment without the people who mean the most to us.
